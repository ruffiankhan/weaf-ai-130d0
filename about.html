The Founder

Hi, I'm Adnan Khan (Addy)

I'm an entrepreneur and AI specialist who has spent years at the intersection of technology and business growth. I've built automation systems, AI-powered tools, and SaaS products -- and I've seen firsthand how AI can fundamentally change how a business operates.

I started WEAF because I noticed a gap: enterprise companies were using AI to save millions, while ambitious entrepreneurs and growing businesses were still running on spreadsheets, voicemail, and manual follow-ups. They were losing revenue every day to problems that AI could solve in a week.

So I built a company that bridges that gap through four pillars: AI Calling Agency Setup, AI Education, AI Automation to Drive Growth, and Agentic AI Acceleration. We don't just hand you a tool and wish you luck. We sit down with you, understand how your business actually runs, and build AI systems that produce measurable outcomes: more revenue, fewer missed opportunities, faster response times, and less wasted time.

When I'm on a strategy call with you, I'm not reading from a script. I'm analyzing your operation and giving you a genuine action plan. That's why I charge $50 for the call -- because the value starts in minute one.
